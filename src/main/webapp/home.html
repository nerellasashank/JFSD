Career Assessment Tool

Overview: The Career Assessment Tool is designed to help students evaluate their skills, interests, and career options. The system aims to provide a user-friendly platform for students to access career assessments, personality tests, and skill evaluations.

Objectives:

• User-friendly interface for students to navigate through various assessment tools.

• Admin functionalities to manage assessments and update test data.

• Offer personalized career recommendations based on assessment results.
